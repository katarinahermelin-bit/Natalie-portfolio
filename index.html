About

I'm a London based creative storyteller with a background in filmmaking, branding, and visual communication. After studying at MET Film School, I developed a passion for emotionally driven storytelling that blends cinematic aesthetics with modern digital culture.

My work sits at the intersection of film, branding, wellness, and human connection. I'm especially drawn to projects that feel immersive, visually atmospheric, and emotionally honest. With experience in creative strategy, social media, and brand storytelling, I approach every project with both cinematic vision and an understanding of how stories connect with audiences today.

Having lived internationally, including time in Japan, I became deeply inspired by Asian culture, minimalism, atmosphere, and the quiet emotional detail often found in everyday moments. The sense of respect, intention, beauty, and visual storytelling within Japanese culture continues to influence the way I see people, environments, and cinematic space.

This international background gave me a deep curiosity about identity, emotion, and the way culture shapes human connection. It continues to inspire both my visual style and the kinds of stories I want to tell.

Alongside filmmaking, I've worked on branding and creative development within the wellness and longevity space, helping shape visual identities, storytelling concepts, and communication strategies for modern health and lifestyle brands.

I'm currently focused on developing work across narrative film, branded content, documentaries, and creative production, with a strong interest in stories that combine beauty, psychology, atmosphere, and emotional depth.
